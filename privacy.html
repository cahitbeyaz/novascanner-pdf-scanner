Privacy Policy for NovaScanner: PDF Scanner

Last Updated: 3 December 2025

1. Introduction

NovaScanner: PDF Scanner (“the App”) is designed to provide document scanning, PDF creation, and text recognition entirely on your device. We take user privacy seriously and this application operates with a strict no data collection policy.

2. No Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal or sensitive data. Specifically, the App does not collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Device information or identifiers
  • Usage or analytics data
  • Location data
  • Contacts, photos, or files
  • Any scanned documents, images, or recognized text

All scanning, processing, and OCR operations occur locally on the user’s device.

3. Camera and Photos Access

The App may request access to the following device features:

Camera

Used solely to scan documents, receipts, notes, or other materials. Camera input is processed on-device and is never sent to a server.

Photos / Files

Accessed only if the user chooses to import an image or document for scanning. Imported files are processed locally and never uploaded, stored externally, or shared.

4. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any:

  • Third-party analytics tools
  • Advertising SDKs
  • Cloud services
  • Tracking technologies

The iOS and Android versions both operate fully offline.

5. Data Sharing

Because the App does not collect any data, no user data is shared with third parties.

6. Children’s Privacy

NovaScanner: PDF Scanner does not collect any information from children or adults. The App is safe for all age groups.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

If we update this policy in the future, the revised version will be posted within the App or on the store listing with a new “Last Updated” date.
